# SEO for Beginners: Get Found on Google

If you've ever wondered why some websites show up at the top of Google and others are buried on page 10, you've already asked the right question. The answer is **SEO — Search Engine Optimization** — and this course will teach you everything you need to know to get started.

## What You'll Learn

This course is your complete beginner's guide to SEO. No prior experience needed. By the end, you'll understand how search engines work, how to research keywords your audience is actually searching for, and how to optimize your pages so Google can find, understand, and rank them.

Here's a quick overview of the four modules:

- **How Search Engines Work** — Understand Google's crawling, indexing, and ranking process from the ground up. Once you know how the machine works, optimizing for it becomes intuitive.
- **Keyword Research** — Discover what your potential visitors are searching for and how to target those searches strategically without blowing a budget on paid tools.
- **On-Page SEO** — Learn to optimize every element of your page: title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, URLs, images, and internal links.
- **Getting Started** — Set up Google Search Console and Google Analytics, submit your sitemap, and start tracking your rankings from day one.

## Who This Course Is For

This course is designed for:

- **Bloggers and content creators** who want more organic traffic without paying for ads
- **Small business owners** who want their websites to show up when customers search
- **Web developers and designers** who want to deliver better results for their clients
- **Students and career changers** exploring digital marketing for the first time

No technical background required. If you can use a web browser and edit a webpage, you have everything you need to get started.

## What You'll Be Able to Do After

After completing this course, you'll be able to:

- Explain how Google crawls, indexes, and ranks web pages
- Conduct keyword research using free tools like Google Search Console and Ubersuggest
- Optimize any web page for SEO — from the title tag to the last paragraph
- Set up and use Google Search Console to monitor your site's performance
- Submit your site to Google and start tracking your rankings week over week

## Why SEO Matters More Than Ever

Paid ads stop working the moment you stop paying. SEO is different — it compounds over time. A well-optimized page can bring in consistent traffic for months or even years without any additional spend.

For developers especially, understanding SEO is a superpower. It means you can build sites that don't just work — they *get found*. You'll write better meta tags, choose cleaner URL structures, optimize images automatically, and deliver results that clients can actually measure.

This course gives you the foundation. Let's get started.
